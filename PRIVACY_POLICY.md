# Privacy Policy for Open in Cursor Plugin

**Last Updated: December 28, 2025**

## Overview

The "Open in Cursor" plugin for IntelliJ IDEA and Android Studio is a simple utility that adds a context menu option to open projects in the Cursor editor. This privacy policy explains our data collection and usage practices.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any user data.**

Specifically, this plugin:
- ❌ Does NOT collect any personal information
- ❌ Does NOT track user behavior or usage statistics
- ❌ Does NOT make any network requests
- ❌ Does NOT store or transmit any data to external servers
- ❌ Does NOT use cookies or tracking technologies
- ❌ Does NOT access or collect project files or code

## What the Plugin Does

The plugin simply:
- ✅ Adds a menu item to the IDE's context menu
- ✅ Executes local commands to open projects in Cursor editor
- ✅ All operations are performed entirely on your local machine

## Local Operations Only

All functionality is performed locally on your computer:
- The plugin detects the Cursor editor installation on your system
- When you click "Open In Cursor", it executes a local command (`cursor <project-path>` or `open -a Cursor <project-path>`)
- No data leaves your machine

## Third-Party Services

This plugin does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, including:
- No analytics services
- No crash reporting services
- No update check services (updates are handled by JetBrains Marketplace)

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data collection practices, we will update this privacy policy and notify users through the plugin marketplace listing.

Since we do not collect any data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete.
